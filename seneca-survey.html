03 — Competitors

What do students use instead?

162 students shared what they use for revision alongside or instead of Seneca. 93% named at least one alternative — often more than one. Students aren't choosing a single competitor; they're assembling portfolios.

üí°

Key insight: Students aren't choosing one competitor — they're assembling revision portfolios. Cognito for video, Quizlet for self-made flashcards, PMT for past papers, Seneca for content recap. A large chunk of "competitors" (Sparx, LanguageNut, Educake) are school-mandated — students didn't choose them. They just eat into available study time. Seneca is losing to an ecosystem, not a single app.
